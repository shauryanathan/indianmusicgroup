This grand concert is held on the 25th and 26th of January of every year and has 3 sessions - an evening session which slowly eases us into the world of Classical Music, a morning concert on the 26th which explores the morning Raagas as the sun rises over the 150 year old Xavier's College Quadrangle, spreading warmth amidst the chilly winter morning followed by a gripping finale on the night of the 26th which leaves us with goosebumps.
